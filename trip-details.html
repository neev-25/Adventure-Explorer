About This Trip

The Himalayan Trek is a once-in-a-lifetime adventure that takes you through some of the most breathtaking landscapes on Earth. This 10-day journey will challenge you physically while rewarding you with stunning views of the world's highest mountains, including Mount Everest.

Our experienced guides will lead you through ancient trails, remote villages, and pristine forests. You'll have the opportunity to immerse yourself in the local culture, meet friendly Sherpa people, and learn about their traditions and way of life.

This trek is suitable for moderately fit individuals who are comfortable walking 5-7 hours per day on varied terrain. No previous trekking experience is required, but a good level of fitness is recommended.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu

Welcome to Nepal! Upon arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport, you'll be transferred to your hotel in Kathmandu. In the evening, we'll have a welcome dinner and trip briefing.

Day 2: Kathmandu to Lukla

Early morning flight to Lukla (2,860m), the gateway to the Everest region. After landing, we'll begin our trek to Phakding (2,610m), a small village on the banks of the Dudh Koshi River.

Day 3: Phakding to Namche Bazaar

Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m), the main trading center of the Khumbu region. The trail follows the Dudh Koshi River and crosses several suspension bridges.

Day 4: Acclimatization Day

Today is an acclimatization day in Namche Bazaar. We'll take a short hike to the Everest View Hotel for panoramic views of the Himalayas, including Mount Everest.

Day 5: Namche Bazaar to Tengboche

Trek to Tengboche (3,870m), home to the largest Buddhist monastery in the Khumbu region. The trail offers stunning views of Ama Dablam, Everest, and other peaks.

Day 6: Tengboche to Dingboche

Continue trekking to Dingboche (4,410m), a beautiful village surrounded by fields and mountains. The trail passes through forests and small settlements.

Day 7: Acclimatization Day

Another acclimatization day in Dingboche. We'll take a short hike to Nangkartshang Peak (5,083m) for panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

Day 8: Dingboche to Lobuche

Trek to Lobuche (4,910m), a small settlement with stunning views of the Khumbu Glacier and surrounding peaks.

Day 9: Lobuche to Gorak Shep and Kala Patthar

Early morning trek to Gorak Shep (5,164m), the last settlement before Everest Base Camp. After lunch, we'll climb Kala Patthar (5,545m) for the best views of Mount Everest.

Day 10: Return to Lukla and Kathmandu

We'll trek back to Lukla and catch a flight back to Kathmandu. In the evening, we'll have a farewell dinner to celebrate our successful trek.
